JOIN Us

If you are interested in joining our team, we would love to hear from you. By becoming a volunteer, you can help support our mission, take part in meaningful projects, and make a real difference in the lives of others. Whether you want to help at events or contribute your skills, every effort counts.

Every bit of time and effort makes a real difference. At Giving Is Living, our volunteers help create opportunities for young people and communities to grow, learn, and feel supported. It is not just about helping out, it is about making a meaningful impact and being part of something bigger.

By volunteering, you can help organise events, support programs, and bring positive change to others. Whether you have a lot of time or just a little, your contribution helps us continue the work we do and reach more people in need.
