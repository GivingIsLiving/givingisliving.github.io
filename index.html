Welcome to
Giving Is Living

We are a not for profit organisation dedicated to supporting young people and any individuals seeking help. Our mission is to create opportunities, provide resources, and build a strong sense of community for those who need it most. We aim to help people by offering guidance, support, and access to essential services that help them grow, succeed, and feel valued.

June 16, 2024

OUR 1000th
CUSTOMER

On June 16th 2024, our organisation successfully helped 1000 young people. That includes jobs, mentoring, therapy, and a whole lot more services.
